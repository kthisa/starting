How God May Send Messages to You Through Dogs

Sometimes people encounter dogs appearing before them to deliver spiritual messages of some kind. They may see angels manifesting in the form of a dog, images of a beloved pet who has died and now they believe is acting as a spirit guide to them, or images of dogs who symbolize something God wants to communicate to them (known as animal totems). Or, they may receive extraordinary inspiration from God simply through their ordinary interactions with the dogs in their lives.
